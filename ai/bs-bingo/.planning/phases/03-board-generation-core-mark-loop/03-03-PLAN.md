<objective>
Build the client-side reactive foundation for Phase 3: the room store gains `board`, `playerMarks`, and `markedCellIds` reactive state with handlers for `boardAssigned` and `wordMarked`, plus a `toggleMark(cellId)` method that does optimistic local flip + `markWord` send. The `BoardCell.svelte` leaf component renders three visual states (unmarked, marked, blank-inert) with proper 44px tap targets and aria semantics.

Purpose: This plan delivers the reactive plumbing and the cell leaf primitive. Plan 04 composes these into the `Board.svelte` grid and wires them into the page. Keeping the store + cell atomic in this plan means the cell can be unit-tested against the store's toggle protocol without needing the full grid mounted.
